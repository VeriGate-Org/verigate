Overview

VeriGate provides identity verification and age-gating solutions for social networks and community platforms. With increasing pressure from regulators and the public to protect minors, prevent impersonation, and reduce fake accounts, social platforms need robust verification that doesn't create prohibitive friction for genuine users.

South Africa's Films and Publications Act and Children's Act impose obligations on platforms hosting user-generated content to prevent minor access to harmful material. VeriGate's social network solution provides age verification, identity confirmation, and account authenticity signals at scale.

Industry Challenges

Minor Protection

Regulatory and public pressure to verify user age and prevent minors from accessing inappropriate content or engaging with adult users.

Fake Accounts

Automated bot accounts and fake profiles erode platform trust, enable harassment, and distort engagement metrics.

User Friction

Verification must be lightweight enough not to deter genuine users from joining, while thorough enough to stop bad actors.
