# VeriGate

**Real-Time Risk Intelligence Platform**
*Cross-agency verification, AI-powered risk scoring, and fraud detection for South Africa -- without a centralised database.*

Built by **Arthmatic DevWorks** | Cape Town, South Africa

---

## The Problem

Verifying a person or company's identity, tax compliance, property ownership, qualifications, and creditworthiness in South Africa requires querying multiple disconnected government and private systems -- DHA, CIPC, SARS, the Deeds Office, SAQA, credit bureaus, and more. The process is manual, slow, error-prone, and riddled with fraud vulnerabilities. There is no unified, secure way to orchestrate these checks at scale.

## The Solution

VeriGate is a real-time risk intelligence platform that routes verification requests to **14+ authoritative sources**, returning standardised results with built-in AI-powered risk scoring, conflict of interest detection, fraud detection, and full audit trails. By cross-referencing CIPC directorship records, shareholding data, sanctions watchlists, property ownership, and PEP databases in real time, VeriGate automatically surfaces hidden relationships -- such as a procurement officer who is also a director of the bidding supplier, or an undisclosed beneficial ownership link between contracting parties. Each agency retains complete control of its own data and infrastructure -- VeriGate orchestrates, never centralises. Deployed on a cross-account AWS model, data sovereignty is guaranteed by architecture, not by policy.

## Key Differentiators

- **Conflict of interest detection** -- automatically cross-references CIPC directorships, shareholding, property ownership, and PEP status to flag undisclosed relationships in procurement, onboarding, and due diligence
- **No centralised database** -- data sovereignty by design; each agency owns its environment
- **Adapter-based architecture** -- plug in any new authoritative source without re-engineering
- **AI/ML risk signals** -- Amazon Bedrock-powered fraud detection and composite risk scoring
- **Visual policy builder** -- partners configure custom verification workflows, no code required
- **Built for SA regulation** -- POPIA-compliant, designed for NCR, FICA, and sector-specific mandates
- **Enterprise-grade stack** -- Java 21, Spring Boot 3.3, AWS serverless, Next.js partner portal

## Market Opportunity

South Africa's KYC, risk intelligence, and background screening market serves every bank, insurer, law firm, property group, and government agency in the country. The global identity verification and risk intelligence market is projected to exceed **USD 21 billion by 2028**. VeriGate is purpose-built for the South African regulatory landscape -- a market with no equivalent real-time risk intelligence platform today.
