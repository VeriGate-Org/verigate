Identity Validation

Real-time ID number validation against the Department of Home Affairs database.

Overview

Identity validation is the foundation of any comprehensive background screening programme. VeriGate's Identity Validation service verifies a candidate's South African ID number in real time against the Department of Home Affairs (DHA) database, confirming that the individual is who they claim to be. Within seconds, you receive confirmation of the candidate's full name, date of birth, citizenship status, and whether the ID number is valid and active.

Beyond basic ID number verification, our platform performs biometric cross-referencing to match the candidate's photo against the DHA's records where available. We also check for deceased status flags, ensuring that the ID number has not been fraudulently appropriated. For organisations subject to FICA, POPIA, or sector-specific KYC requirements, our identity validation service provides the first line of defence against identity fraud and non-compliance, with results available in under 60 seconds.

How It Works

1

Submit ID Number

Enter the candidate's 13-digit South African ID number through the VeriGate portal, API, or bulk upload facility. The system performs an instant format validation before querying the database.

2

DHA Database Query

The ID number is verified in real time against the Department of Home Affairs national population register, confirming the individual's identity, citizenship status, and ID document validity.

3

Biometric Cross-Reference

Where available, the candidate's photograph is cross-referenced against the DHA's biometric records to confirm a visual match, adding an additional layer of identity assurance.

4

Instant Validation

Results are returned to your dashboard in under 60 seconds, including a confirmed identity match, deceased status check, and a confidence score for biometric matching where applicable.

Key Features

DHA Database Verification

Direct integration with the Department of Home Affairs national population register provides authoritative identity confirmation. We verify full names, date of birth, gender, citizenship status, and ID document validity in real time.

Photo ID Matching

Our biometric cross-referencing technology matches the candidate's photo against DHA records where available, providing visual confirmation of identity and an additional safeguard against impersonation or identity fraud.

Deceased Status Check

Every identity validation includes an automatic deceased status check against the DHA death register. This critical check detects fraudulent use of deceased individuals' identity numbers, protecting your organisation from identity theft.

Turnaround Time

Real-time (under 60 seconds)

Identity validation results are returned instantly, typically within 5 to 15 seconds. Biometric cross-referencing may take up to 60 seconds depending on database availability.
