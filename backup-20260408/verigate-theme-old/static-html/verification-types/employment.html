Employment History Verification

Confirm employment dates, roles, and performance with previous employers.

Overview

VeriGate's Employment History Verification service provides a thorough review of a candidate's professional background by contacting previous employers directly. We confirm employment dates, job titles, reporting lines, key responsibilities, and reason for leaving. This service is essential for identifying CV discrepancies, inflated titles, or fabricated employment periods that could indicate a candidate's unsuitability for a role.

Our verification team uses a structured methodology that includes multiple contact attempts through HR departments, line managers, and payroll records. Where employers have closed or been acquired, we utilise alternative verification methods including UIF records, company registration databases, and industry networks. Each verification is documented with the name and contact details of the confirming party, providing a robust audit trail for your records and compliance requirements.

How It Works

1

Submit Employment History

Upload the candidate's employment history through the VeriGate portal, including company names, dates of employment, job titles held, and contact details for references where available.

2

Contact Previous Employers

Our verification team contacts each listed employer through official HR channels, making multiple attempts via phone, email, and written correspondence to obtain confirmation.

3

Verify Dates and Roles

We confirm start and end dates, job title, department, reporting structure, key responsibilities, reason for leaving, and eligibility for re-hire where the employer is willing to disclose.

4

Comprehensive Report

A detailed employment verification report is delivered to your dashboard, highlighting any discrepancies between the candidate's claims and the employer's confirmation, with a clear summary of findings.

Key Features

Role and Date Confirmation

We verify exact employment dates, job titles, and responsibilities with each previous employer. Any discrepancies between the candidate's CV and the employer's records are clearly flagged in the report for your review.

Reason for Leaving

Where employers are willing to disclose, we capture the reason for leaving, whether the candidate resigned, was retrenched, dismissed, or their contract expired. This provides critical context for your hiring decision.

Performance References

Beyond basic employment confirmation, we gather performance-related feedback where available, including eligibility for re-hire, conduct record, and general performance assessment from line managers or HR representatives.

Turnaround Time

2–4 business days

Standard employment verifications are completed within 2 to 4 business days. Turnaround may vary depending on employer responsiveness and the number of positions being verified.
