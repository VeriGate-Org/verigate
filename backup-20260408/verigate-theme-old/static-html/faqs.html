Frequently Asked Questions

VeriGate offers comprehensive verification services including criminal record checks, identity verification, qualification verification, employment history, credit screening, face verification, document verification, business verification, address verification, age verification, eIDV, and video identification. We also provide compliance solutions (KYC, KYB, AML screening) and fraud prevention tools.
Turnaround times vary by verification type. Automated checks like eIDV and face verification complete in seconds. Criminal record checks take 24-48 hours. Employment and qualification verifications typically take 2-5 business days depending on the responsiveness of the employer or institution.
Yes, VeriGate is fully compliant with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). We process all personal information in accordance with the conditions for lawful processing, maintain appropriate security safeguards, and provide tools for consent management, data subject access requests, and breach notification.
VeriGate offers a comprehensive REST API with 50+ endpoints, pre-built SDKs for Python, Node.js, PHP, and Java, webhook notifications, and out-of-the-box integrations with popular HR platforms, ATS systems, and CRM tools. Most integrations are completed within 1-2 days.
VeriGate uses a simple per-verification pricing model with three tiers: Starter (R29/verification, up to 100/month), Professional (R22/verification, up to 1,000/month), and Enterprise (custom pricing for unlimited verifications). All plans include platform access and API.
Yes, VeriGate supports identity documents from 195 countries including passports, national ID cards, and driving licences. For South African documents, we offer the deepest verification including DHA database cross-referencing and NFC chip reading.
VeriGate supports FICA (KYC/CDD/EDD), POPIA, AML screening (FIC Act), the Companies Act (KYB via CIPC), FSCA regulations, SARB prudential standards, ISO 27001, and SOC 2. Our compliance modules are modular — use what you need.
VeriGate maintains a 99.2% overall accuracy rate across all verification types. Our face verification achieves 99.6% match accuracy, document verification has a 99.4% forgery detection rate, and our eIDV returns results with 99.8% data extraction accuracy.
Starter and Professional plans are month-to-month with no minimum commitment. Enterprise plans typically involve an annual agreement with negotiated pricing based on volume. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time.
VeriGate implements bank-grade security including AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit, SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure, ISO 27001 aligned processes, role-based access control, audit logging, and regular penetration testing. We are hosted in South African data centres to ensure data sovereignty.
All plans include email support. Professional plans add phone support with 4-hour response times. Enterprise plans include a dedicated account manager, 24/7 support with 1-hour response times for critical issues, and quarterly business reviews.
Yes, our platform supports bulk uploads via CSV and batch API calls. Enterprise clients routinely process thousands of verifications per batch. Our infrastructure auto-scales to handle any volume without performance degradation.
