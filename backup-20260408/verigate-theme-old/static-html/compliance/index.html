Compliance Solutions

Stay compliant with South Africa's regulatory requirements. From KYC and AML to POPIA and ISO 27001, VeriGate provides comprehensive compliance tools.

KYC

Know Your Customer identity verification for FICA compliance.

Comprehensive customer identity verification using DHA databases, document authentication, and biometric matching — fully compliant with FICA and FIC Act requirements.

KYB

Company registration verification, director screening, BEE status checks, and beneficial ownership analysis through CIPC and other authoritative sources.

Investor identity verification, source of funds checks, PEP screening, and sanctions list monitoring for collective investment schemes and platforms.

Real-time screening against global sanctions lists, PEP databases, and enforcement actions as required by the Financial Intelligence Centre Act.
