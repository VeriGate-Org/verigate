Credit Screening

Consumer credit profile checks for financial risk assessment and compliance.

Overview

VeriGate's Credit Screening service provides comprehensive consumer credit profile assessments for organisations that need to evaluate the financial standing of candidates, employees, or business partners. By integrating directly with South Africa's leading credit bureaus -- TransUnion and Experian -- we deliver detailed credit reports that include credit scores, payment history, outstanding debt, judgements, defaults, and administration orders. This information is critical for roles involving financial responsibility, fiduciary duties, or access to sensitive financial systems.

All credit checks are conducted in strict compliance with the National Credit Act (NCA), the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), and applicable employment legislation. Candidate consent is verified before any credit bureau query is initiated, and all reports are delivered through our secure, encrypted platform. Our credit screening service is particularly valuable for financial services, banking, insurance, and any organisation where employees handle funds, procurement, or financial decision-making.

How It Works

1

Submit Candidate Consent

Upload the candidate's signed consent form along with their ID number through the VeriGate portal. Our system verifies that valid consent has been obtained before proceeding, as required by the NCA and POPIA.

2

Credit Bureau Query

We query TransUnion and Experian databases to retrieve the candidate's full consumer credit profile, including credit accounts, payment history, outstanding balances, and any adverse records.

3

Compile Credit Profile

The raw credit data is compiled into a clear, structured report that highlights key indicators including credit score, debt-to-income ratio, judgements, defaults, and administration or sequestration orders.

4

Risk Assessment Report

A comprehensive risk assessment report is delivered to your dashboard, including an overall financial risk rating, detailed credit profile summary, and flagged items requiring attention based on your organisation's screening policy.

Key Features

TransUnion/Experian Integration

Direct integration with South Africa's two largest credit bureaus ensures comprehensive coverage. We query both TransUnion and Experian to provide the most complete credit profile available, capturing accounts and records across all credit providers.

Credit Score and History

Every credit screening report includes the candidate's current credit score, full payment history across all credit accounts, outstanding debt levels, and a debt-to-income analysis where salary information is available.

Judgements and Defaults

We identify and report all civil judgements, defaults, adverse listings, administration orders, and sequestration records. Each item includes the creditor name, amount, date, and current status for complete transparency.

Turnaround Time

Real-time to 24 hours

Basic credit score checks are returned in real time. Comprehensive credit profile reports with full risk assessments are delivered within 24 hours.
