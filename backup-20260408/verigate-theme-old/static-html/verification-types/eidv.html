Overview

VeriGate's Electronic Identity Verification (eIDV) confirms an individual's identity instantly by cross-referencing their personal details against multiple authoritative South African data sources — including the Department of Home Affairs, credit bureaus, telecommunications providers, and banking databases. No physical document is required.

eIDV is ideal for low-friction onboarding scenarios where speed is critical and the risk profile is moderate. By verifying that a name, ID number, date of birth, and address are consistent across multiple independent databases, eIDV provides strong identity assurance without the need for document uploads or biometric capture.

How It Works

1

Data Input

The applicant provides their full name, SA ID number, date of birth, and residential address through your onboarding form or API call.

2

Multi-Source Query

VeriGate simultaneously queries the DHA database, credit bureaus (TransUnion, Experian), telco records, and voter's roll for matching records.

3

Consistency Analysis

Our engine analyses the consistency of returned data across sources, calculating a confidence score based on the number and quality of matches.

4

Verification Result

A pass/refer/fail result is returned instantly with a detailed breakdown of which sources matched and the overall confidence level.

Key Features

Multi-Source Verification

Cross-reference identity data across 5+ authoritative South African databases for comprehensive, document-free identity confirmation.

Instant Results

eIDV returns results in under 3 seconds, enabling real-time onboarding without document uploads or manual processing.

FICA Compliant

Meets the FIC's requirements for electronic verification of customer identity under the risk-based approach to CDD.

Turnaround Time

Under 3 seconds

eIDV is fully automated and returns results in 1-3 seconds for standard queries.
