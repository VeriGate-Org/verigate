Device Intelligence

Overview

VeriGate's Device Intelligence service analyses the device used during identity verification to detect fraud signals. By examining device characteristics, network configuration, geolocation, and behavioural patterns, we identify high-risk devices, emulators, rooted phones, VPN usage, and known fraud toolkits — adding a critical layer to your fraud prevention strategy.

Fraudsters often use specific tools to defeat identity verification — device emulators, GPS spoofers, VPNs, and automation frameworks. Our device intelligence SDK detects these tools in real time, flagging suspicious devices before the verification process even begins.

How It Works

1

SDK Integration

Integrate our lightweight JavaScript or mobile SDK into your application. The SDK silently collects device signals during the user session.

2

Device Fingerprinting

Create a unique device fingerprint from 100+ attributes including hardware specs, OS version, screen resolution, installed fonts, and WebGL renderer.

3

Risk Signal Analysis

Evaluate device risk signals — emulator detection, root/jailbreak status, VPN usage, GPS spoofing, automation tools, and known fraud device database.

4

Risk Score & Action

A device risk score is returned with specific risk signals identified, enabling automated blocking, step-up verification, or manual review.

Key Features

100+ Device Attributes

Analyse hardware, software, network, and behavioral signals to create a comprehensive device risk profile and unique fingerprint.

Fraud Tool Detection

Detect device emulators, rooted/jailbroken devices, GPS spoofers, VPNs, automation frameworks, and screen-sharing software.

Device Reputation

Cross-reference devices against VeriGate's global device reputation database of previously flagged devices linked to fraud attempts.

Turnaround Time

Real-time

Device intelligence analysis runs in the background during the user session, with risk scores available in under 2 seconds.
