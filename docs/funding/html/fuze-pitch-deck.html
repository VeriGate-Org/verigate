Fragmented Systems

04 — Architecture
Data sovereignty by design, not by policy.
Each agency retains full control of its own data. VeriGate orchestrates, never centralises.
Adapter-Based Architecture
Every verification source is a self-contained adapter module. Adding a new country means building adapters for local registries — not rebuilding the platform. This is how VeriGate scales across Africa without proportional engineering cost.
Cross-Account AWS Model
Each participating agency gets its own AWS account. VeriGate orchestrates verification across accounts without ever centralising raw data. This is a hard requirement for government adoption and extraordinarily difficult to retrofit.
Event-Driven Audit Trail
Every verification is an immutable event — fully auditable, replayable, and analyzable. Meets POPIA, FICA, and NCR compliance requirements.
