About VeriGate

We are on a mission to make background verification in South Africa faster, more accurate, and fully compliant -- empowering organisations to build trust with confidence.

Our Story

VeriGate was founded in 2020 with a clear vision: to modernize the background verification landscape in South Africa. Our founders recognised that organisations across the country were struggling with a fragmented, slow, and often unreliable verification ecosystem that left them exposed to risk and compliance failures.

The South African verification industry was built on manual processes, disconnected data sources, and lengthy turnaround times. Employers, financial institutions, and government bodies needed a unified platform that could deliver accurate results quickly while maintaining strict adherence to POPIA and other regulatory frameworks.

Today, VeriGate serves over 200 enterprise clients across multiple industries, processing more than 50,000 verifications with a 99.2% accuracy rate. Our technology-driven approach combines automated data retrieval with expert human oversight, ensuring every report meets the highest standards of reliability and compliance.

Our Mission & Values

These core principles guide everything we do -- from how we build our platform to how we serve our clients.

Accuracy

Every verification is thorough and precise. We cross-reference multiple authoritative sources and apply rigorous quality assurance to ensure our clients receive results they can trust completely.

Speed

Our technology-driven processes deliver fast turnaround times without compromising on quality. Automated data retrieval, intelligent routing, and real-time status tracking keep your operations moving.

Compliance

Full adherence to POPIA, the National Credit Act, and all relevant South African regulations. Our platform is designed from the ground up to protect personal information and meet every regulatory requirement.
