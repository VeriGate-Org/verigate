Overview

VeriGate's Synthetic Identity Detection identifies fabricated identities that combine real data elements (like stolen ID numbers) with fictitious personal details. Synthetic identity fraud is the fastest-growing type of identity fraud in South Africa, costing financial institutions billions annually because these identities pass basic verification checks.

Our solution goes beyond standard identity verification by analysing the consistency and history of identity elements across multiple data sources. A synthetic identity may have a valid ID number but no credit history, an address that doesn't match any records, or a phone number recently activated — our system detects these inconsistencies.

How It Works

1

Identity Element Collection

Capture all available identity elements — ID number, name, date of birth, address, phone number, email, and employment details.

2

Cross-Source Consistency

Each element is checked for consistency across credit bureaus, telco records, DHA data, voter's roll, and property registries.

3

History & Footprint Analysis

Analyse the identity's digital and financial footprint — credit history length, address stability, phone number age, and social presence.

4

Synthetic Risk Score

A synthetic identity risk score is generated based on the number and severity of consistency gaps, enabling risk-based decisions.

Key Features

Cross-Source Analysis

Validate identity elements across 7+ independent data sources to detect inconsistencies that indicate synthetic fabrication.

Footprint Scoring

Assess the depth and consistency of an identity's digital, financial, and social footprint to distinguish real from fabricated identities.

Pattern Recognition

Detect common synthetic identity patterns — new credit files with high activity, address clustering, and coordinated application patterns.

Turnaround Time

Under 30 seconds

Synthetic identity analysis completes in 15-30 seconds, running alongside standard verification checks.
