Overview

VeriGate helps cryptocurrency exchanges, DeFi platforms, and virtual asset service providers (VASPs) meet South Africa's evolving crypto regulations. Following the FSCA's declaration of crypto assets as financial products, and the FIC's inclusion of crypto platforms as accountable institutions, robust KYC and AML compliance is now mandatory.

Our crypto-specific solution addresses the unique challenges of the digital asset space — pseudonymous users, cross-border transactions, blockchain analytics integration, and the FATF Travel Rule — while maintaining the fast onboarding experience that crypto users expect.

Industry Challenges

New Regulatory Requirements

FSCA licensing and FIC Act compliance require comprehensive KYC for all crypto users — a major operational shift for many platforms.

Travel Rule Compliance

The FATF Travel Rule requires originator and beneficiary information to travel with virtual asset transfers above R5,000.

High Fraud Risk

Crypto platforms face elevated identity fraud risk from users seeking anonymity for illicit purposes, including money laundering and sanctions evasion.

Our Solutions

Crypto KYC

Tiered KYC workflows aligned with risk — simplified verification for low-value users, enhanced due diligence for high-volume traders and large transactions.

AML & Sanctions Screening

Screen all users against global sanctions lists and PEP databases, with ongoing monitoring and automated re-screening for regulatory compliance.

Travel Rule Compliance

Automated originator and beneficiary data exchange for virtual asset transfers, ensuring compliance with FATF Travel Rule requirements.
