Face Verification

Overview

VeriGate's Face Verification service uses advanced biometric technology to match a live selfie against an identity document photo. Our AI-powered liveness detection ensures the person is physically present, preventing spoofing attempts with printed photos, screen replays, or masks.

Designed for remote onboarding and high-security access control, our facial matching engine achieves a 99.6% match accuracy rate. The system supports South African smart ID cards, green barcoded IDs, passports, and driving licences, with results returned in under 10 seconds.

How It Works

1

Capture Selfie

The user takes a live selfie using their device camera. Our SDK guides them through optimal positioning and lighting for best results.

2

Liveness Detection

AI algorithms analyse the selfie for liveness indicators — blinking, micro-movements, and 3D depth — to confirm a real person is present.

3

Document Photo Extraction

We extract the photo from the submitted identity document using OCR and image processing, normalising for angle and lighting differences.

4

Biometric Matching

Our facial recognition engine compares 128 biometric data points between the selfie and document photo, returning a confidence score and match result.

Key Features

Liveness Detection

Passive and active liveness checks prevent presentation attacks including printed photos, video replays, deepfakes, and 3D masks.

Sub-10-Second Results

Get face verification results in under 10 seconds, enabling seamless real-time onboarding without delays or manual review.

POPIA Compliant

Biometric data is processed in compliance with POPIA and the Cybercrimes Act. Facial templates are encrypted and not stored beyond verification.

Turnaround Time

Under 10 seconds

Face verification is fully automated and returns results in real time, typically within 5-10 seconds.
