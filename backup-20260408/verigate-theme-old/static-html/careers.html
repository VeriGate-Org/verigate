Careers at VeriGate

Join our mission to make identity verification faster, more accurate, and fully compliant for every organisation in Africa.

Our Culture

At VeriGate, we believe the best work happens when talented people are given autonomy, trust, and the tools to build remarkable things. Our team is distributed across South Africa with a home base in Johannesburg, and we gather regularly for team events, hackathons, and strategy sessions.

Why Work at VeriGate

VeriGate is building the verification infrastructure that South Africa's digital economy depends on. Every day, our technology helps organisations make confident decisions about the people and businesses they work with. We're a team of engineers, data scientists, compliance experts, and product builders who believe that trust should be easy to establish and impossible to fake.

Based in Johannesburg with remote-friendly policies, we offer competitive compensation, equity participation, medical aid contributions, and a learning budget for every team member. We value ownership, curiosity, and impact over credentials and job titles.

Open Positions

We're always looking for talented people to join our team. Current openings include:

  • Senior Full-Stack Engineer — Build and scale our verification platform using Python, React, and PostgreSQL. Experience with biometric systems or OCR is a plus.
  • Machine Learning Engineer — Develop and improve our document fraud detection, facial matching, and deepfake detection models using PyTorch and TensorFlow.
  • Compliance Analyst — Help our clients navigate FICA, POPIA, and FSCA requirements. Deep knowledge of South African financial regulation required.
  • Product Designer — Design intuitive interfaces for complex verification workflows. Experience with fintech or regtech products preferred.
  • Sales Development Representative — Help South African organisations discover how VeriGate can transform their verification processes.

Our Values

  • Trust by Default — We build systems that make trust the default, not the exception. Every feature we ship increases confidence in identity.
  • Speed Without Shortcuts — We move fast but never compromise on accuracy, security, or compliance. Quality is non-negotiable.
  • Customer Obsession — Our clients' compliance is our compliance. We succeed when they succeed.
  • Continuous Learning — The verification landscape evolves constantly. We invest in learning and expect every team member to grow.
